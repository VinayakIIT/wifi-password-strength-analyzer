How to Improve Your Password

  • Increase length: Aim for at least 12 characters
  • Add variety: Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid patterns: Don't use sequential numbers (1234) or repeated characters (aaaa)
  • Avoid personal info: Don't use names, birthdays, or common words
  • Be unique: Don't reuse passwords across multiple networks
